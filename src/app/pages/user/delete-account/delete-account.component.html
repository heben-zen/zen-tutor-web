Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Log in to your Zen Shorts account.
  2. Navigate to your profile.
  3. Click on the "More options" button.
  4. Click on the "Delete Account" button.

Data Deletion Information

When you request to delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact information.

Note: Some data may be retained for a certain period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
